Blog Refresh

Published: Wed 04 April 2018
By nightblade

In News.

After a few months of stagnation on the blogging/marketing side, we're finally back! We hope, inshaAllah (God willing) to publish regular (or at least semi-regular) updates to our blog and our new maintainer's Twitter account.

Accordingly, we also ditched our old, homebrew-coded blog for something sleek and production-grade (built in Pelican) blog.

Enjoy, and as usual, hit us up on Twitter to let us know what you think.
